FAQ

  • How do I start playing?
    Download the game from Google Play, launch the app, and follow the introduction tutorial.
  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes, the main storyline is free, with optional premium content.
  • Are there in-app purchases?
    Yes, you can buy bonus artifacts, extra energy, and exclusive storyline elements.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes! The main campaign can be played without an internet connection.
  • How often are new chapters released?
    Our team regularly updates the game with fresh content, events, and quests.
